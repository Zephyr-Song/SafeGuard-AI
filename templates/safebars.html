SafeBARS Research Rehearsal

Bias-aware synthetic stakeholders for pre-fieldwork action research planning.

Use this prototype to rehearse a sensitive research plan before fieldwork. The synthetic stakeholders are fictional prompts for reflection, not substitutes for real participants or community partners.
Study mode: draft an initial plan, rehearse it with a fictional stakeholder, generate a reflection report, then write what you would revise before real fieldwork.
