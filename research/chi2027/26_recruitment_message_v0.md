## Short English Version

Hi, I am recruiting participants for a formative study of SafeBARS, a prototype that helps researchers rehearse sensitive action research plans with fictional synthetic stakeholders.

The study is about research planning, not about your personal experiences. You will draft a short research plan, try the prototype, revise the plan, and answer a few interview questions.

The session takes about 60 minutes. I am looking for people with experience or training in HCI, UX research, design research, AI safety, CSCW, or related areas.

If you are interested, please let me know. Thank you!

## Email Version

Subject: Recruiting participants for a formative study on AI-assisted research planning

Hi [Name],

I am recruiting participants for a formative study of SafeBARS, a prototype that helps researchers rehearse sensitive action research plans with fictional synthetic stakeholders.

In the study, you will:

1. Draft a short action research plan.
2. Use the SafeBARS prototype to rehearse the plan.
3. Revise the plan.
4. Join a short interview about your experience.

The session will take about 60 minutes. The study is about research planning and prototype feedback; it does not ask you to share personal experiences with fraud or harm.

I am looking for participants with experience or training in HCI, UX research, design research, AI safety, CSCW, or related areas.

Please let me know if you are interested or have any questions.

Best,

[Your Name]

## Recruitment Criteria

Include:

- Age 18+.
- HCI, UX, design research, AI safety, CSCW, or related background.
- Comfortable discussing research planning and ethics.

Avoid for first formative study:

- People who would be recruited as affected participants in the final online fraud prevention research.
- People who would need to share personal fraud experiences.
