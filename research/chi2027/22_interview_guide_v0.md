# SafeBARS Interview Guide v0

Last updated: 2026-06-16

## Status

Draft for formative study planning.

## Opening

Thank you for trying the prototype. I am interested in how this system supports research planning, not in evaluating your personal research ability.

## Warm-Up

1. Can you briefly describe your experience with user research, design research, HCI, AI safety, or related work?
2. Have you used LLMs to brainstorm, role-play, or prepare research materials before?
3. If yes, what did you find useful or risky about that?

## Initial Plan Reflection

4. Before using the prototype, what were you mainly thinking about when preparing the research plan?
5. What risks or challenges did you already expect in this kind of fieldwork?

## Prototype Experience

6. What did you do first in SafeBARS, and why?
7. Which stakeholder response stood out to you?
8. Did any response make you rethink part of your research plan?
9. Did the bias/risk-response profile feel understandable?
10. Did adjusting or seeing the profile help you think differently about the stakeholder?

## Ethical and Safety Risk Discovery

11. Did the system help you notice any ethical or safety risks?
12. Did it surface any privacy, distress, consent, or participant burden concerns?
13. Did it make you notice any language that could sound blaming or judgmental?
14. Did it reveal any missing stakeholders or relationships?

## Trust Calibration

15. How did you interpret the synthetic stakeholder's response?
16. Did it feel like evidence, inspiration, a warning, or something else?
17. Were there moments when you thought the synthetic stakeholder might be misleading?
18. What would you still need to verify with real participants or community partners?

## Revised Plan

19. What did you change in your revised plan?
20. Why did you make those changes?
21. Which changes came directly from the rehearsal?
22. Which parts of the plan are still uncertain?

## System Feedback

23. What part of the system was most useful?
24. What part was confusing or unnecessary?
25. What stakeholder roles should be added?
26. What bias or risk-response dimensions should be added, removed, or renamed?
27. What should the reflection report do better?

## Closing

28. Would you use a tool like this before conducting sensitive fieldwork? Why or why not?
29. What would make it safer or more trustworthy?
30. Is there anything else you want to add?

## Optional Follow-Up Probes

- Can you show me where in your revised plan this changed?
- What made that response feel realistic or unrealistic?
- Did the limitation reminders affect how you used the system?
- Did the system make you more cautious, or just more confident?
- Would this be useful for supervisors or ethics review preparation?
