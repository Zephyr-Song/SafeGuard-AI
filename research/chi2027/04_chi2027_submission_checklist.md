## Before Study

- [ ] Confirm research framing with supervisor.
- [ ] Decide whether ethics/IRB approval or exemption is required.
- [ ] Prepare consent form.
- [ ] Prepare recruitment message.
- [ ] Prepare study protocol.
- [ ] Prepare interview guide.
- [ ] Prepare data management plan.
- [ ] Prepare distress and withdrawal procedure.
- [ ] Prepare anonymization plan.

## Before Prototype Study

- [ ] Prototype runs locally.
- [ ] No real participant sensitive data is required for demo tasks.
- [ ] Interaction logs are anonymized or pseudonymized.
- [ ] System displays limitations of synthetic stakeholders.
- [ ] System does not claim to represent real communities.
- [ ] Safety warnings are visible where needed.
- [ ] Researcher-facing reflection report is exportable.

## Paper Package

- [ ] Paper in CHI single-column submission format.
- [ ] Recommended word count checked.
- [ ] Title anonymized.
- [ ] Author names removed.
- [ ] Institution names removed.
- [ ] Repository links anonymized or removed.
- [ ] Acknowledgements removed during review.
- [ ] Metadata checked.
- [ ] Figures anonymized.
- [ ] Supplementary materials anonymized.
- [ ] Demo video anonymized.
- [ ] Ethics note included if required by submission form.
- [ ] AI use policy checked and documented.

## Empirical Evidence Checklist

- [ ] Clear participant sample.
- [ ] Clear recruitment criteria.
- [ ] Clear study task.
- [ ] Pre/post artifacts collected.
- [ ] Interaction logs collected.
- [ ] Interview or think-aloud data collected.
- [ ] Coding process documented.
- [ ] At least two researchers review qualitative themes if possible.
- [ ] Limitations stated honestly.

## Contribution Checklist

The paper must clearly answer:

- [ ] What is the new HCI problem?
- [ ] Why is ordinary LLM persona simulation insufficient?
- [ ] How does the work build on CoBRA without duplicating it?
- [ ] What does the system do?
- [ ] What did the study show?
- [ ] Why does this matter for responsible AI and action research?
- [ ] What should future AI research tools learn from this project?

## Review Risk Checklist

Potential reviewer concerns:

- [ ] Is this just a prompt engineering tool?
- [ ] Is this just CoBRA applied to fraud?
- [ ] Is this just ChatGPT role-play?
- [ ] Does it wrongly replace real participants?
- [ ] Is the study too small?
- [ ] Are the findings too obvious?
- [ ] Are the bias scores validated?
- [ ] Does the system overclaim psychological validity?

Planned response:

- Frame the system as pre-fieldwork rehearsal, not participant replacement.
- Emphasize reflection, trust calibration, and research safety.
- Keep claims about bias profiles functional and behavioral, not psychological.
- Use formative evidence to show how researchers revise plans and surface risks.
