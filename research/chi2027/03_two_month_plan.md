# Two-Month CHI 2027 Execution Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-16

Goal: produce a CHI-ready project package by mid-August 2026, leaving late August and early September for paper revision, anonymization, supplementary materials, and submission.

Official CHI 2027 paper deadline: 2026-09-10 AoE

Official papers page:
https://chi2027.acm.org/authors/papers/

## Phase 1: Problem Framing and Research Setup

Dates: 2026-06-16 to 2026-06-23

Status: in progress

Deliverables:

- Project brief.
- CoBRA transfer analysis.
- Literature matrix seed.
- Research questions.
- CHI submission checklist.
- Codex workflow.

Codex tasks:

- Maintain planning files.
- Expand literature matrix.
- Draft supervisor updates.
- Identify novelty risks.

Human tasks:

- Confirm framing with supervisor.
- Decide whether the main domain should be online fraud, AI safety education, or broader high-risk digital safety.

Definition of done:

- Supervisor agrees the project should move from end-user anti-fraud education to researcher-facing action research scaffolding.

## Phase 2: Literature Review and Contribution Lock

Dates: 2026-06-24 to 2026-06-30

Deliverables:

- 25-40 paper literature matrix.
- Related work map.
- Finalized RQ set.
- Contribution paragraph v1.
- Novelty risk memo.

Codex tasks:

- Summarize papers into the matrix.
- Compare SafeBARS against CoBRA, synthetic users, ROLESafe, and generative agents.
- Draft related work skeleton.

Human tasks:

- Read the most important 8-10 papers personally.
- Mark which papers are central, supporting, or threat-to-novelty.

Definition of done:

- One-page argument explaining why SafeBARS is not just CoBRA, not just ROLESafe, and not just ChatGPT for brainstorming.

Deliverables:

- System architecture.
- Bias profile schema.
- Stakeholder agent schema.
- Rehearsal workflow.
- Reflection dashboard specification.
- Prototype issue list.

Codex tasks:

- Draft JSON schemas.
- Create module plan for adapting existing SafeGuard-AI code.
- Draft prompt templates for stakeholder agents.
- Draft dashboard output format.

Human tasks:

- Select 3-4 initial stakeholder roles.
- Select 4-6 initial bias/risk-response dimensions.
- Confirm the first study task scenario.

Definition of done:

Deliverables:

- Reflection report output.

Codex tasks:

Human tasks:

## Phase 6: Formative Study

Dates: 2026-07-24 to 2026-08-02

Participants:

- 6-8 HCI, UX, CSCW, AI safety, or design research students/researchers.

Study flow:

1. Consent and introduction.
2. Initial action research plan.
3. SafeBARS rehearsal.
4. Revised action research plan.
5. Interview.

Data:

- Pre-plan.
- Interaction logs.
- Reflection report.
- Revised plan.
- Interview transcript or notes.

Codex tasks:

- Generate session summaries.
- Compare pre/post plans.
- Apply first-pass coding.

Human tasks:

- Conduct sessions.
- Write immediate field notes after each session.

Definition of done:

- All sessions summarized and initial themes identified.

## Phase 7: Prototype v2 and Pilot Evaluation

Dates: 2026-08-03 to 2026-08-10

Deliverables:

- Revised prototype.
- Pilot study design.
- Comparison condition: ordinary LLM brainstorming.
- Evaluation rubric.
- Preliminary results.

Possible pilot:

- N = 12-18.
- Compare ordinary LLM brainstorming with SafeBARS.
- Outcome measures:
  - Number and quality of ethical risks identified.
  - Stakeholder coverage.
  - Safety plan completeness.
  - Research plan revision quality.
  - Trust calibration.
  - Perceived reflection support.

Definition of done:

- Enough empirical material to support a CHI paper draft, even if the pilot remains small.
