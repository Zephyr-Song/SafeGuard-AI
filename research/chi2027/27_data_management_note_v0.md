# Data Management Note v0

Last updated: 2026-06-16

Status: Draft for supervisor review. Adapt to institutional ethics requirements before use.

## Study Data Types

The formative study may collect:

- Participant ID.
- Initial research plan.
- SafeBARS interaction logs.
- Reflection report generated by the prototype.
- Revised research plan.
- Interview notes or transcript.
- Optional audio or screen recording if consented.

## Data Minimization

The study should avoid collecting:

- Real names of affected people or community members.
- Personal fraud experiences.
- Financial details.
- Sensitive third-party information.
- Unnecessary demographic details.

Participants should be reminded not to include real personal or third-party identifying information in their research plans.

## Participant IDs

Use participant IDs such as:

- P01
- P02
- P03

Do not use real names in analysis files.

## File Organization

Recommended folder structure:

```text
research/data/
  raw/
    P01/
    P02/
  anonymized/
    P01_summary.md
    P02_summary.md
  analysis/
    coding_table.xlsx
    themes.md
```

Raw identifiable data should not be committed to a public repository.

## Prototype Logs

SafeBARS exports JSON and Markdown session files.

Before using logs for analysis:

- Check for accidental names or personal details.
- Replace participant names with participant IDs.
- Remove unnecessary identifiers.
- Mark whether the output is initial plan, interaction log, reflection report, or revised plan.

## Audio or Screen Recording

If recording is used:

- Ask for explicit consent.
- Store recordings separately from anonymized analysis files.
- Delete recordings after transcription if required by ethics protocol.
- Do not include recordings in supplements.

## Anonymization

Before analysis or paper writing:

- Replace names with participant IDs.
- Remove institution names if identifying.
- Paraphrase details that could identify a participant.
- Remove timestamps if unnecessary.

Before CHI submission:

- Ensure supplementary materials are anonymized.
- Remove researcher/institution metadata.
- Avoid repository links that identify authors during review.

## Retention

Retention period should follow institutional policy and supervisor guidance.

Draft placeholder:

- Raw data retained until analysis is complete or according to approved protocol.
- Anonymized data retained for publication support if approved.

## Access Control

Only the research team should access raw data.

Do not share raw data through public links, public repositories, or unsecured messaging.

## AI Use with Study Data

If using AI tools to summarize or analyze data:

- Use anonymized data only.
- Do not include participant names or identifying details.
- Keep a log of AI assistance.
- Verify AI-generated summaries against original data.
